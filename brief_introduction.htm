Research

Dr. Wu's research focuses on service quality, decision analysis, data mining, quality management, and applied statistics in recent years. He has published 187 journal papers (the 100th paper has been accepted on November 23, 2011) since 1999. He currently serves an associate editor of International Journal of Management, Economics and Social Sciences (indexed in Inspec) since August 25, 2015 and IISE Transactions on Healthcare Systems Engineering since June 17, 2016. He was an editorial board member of Journal of Industrial Engineering (indexed in Inspec) from July 4, 2012 to November 13, 2017 as well as an editorial board member in Computer Science of The Scientific World Journal (indexed in Science Citation Index Expanded) from December 2013 to August 2016. He joined the editorial board of Journal of Quality (indexed in Compendex and Scopus) as focus issue editor in total quality management and business excellence from July 2010 to December 2011, was an editor-in-chief from January 2012 to December 2012, and becomes advisory board member since January 1, 2013. Since September 7, 2016, Dr. Wu becomes an editorial board member of Journal of Industrial and Intelligent Information (indexed in Inspec). More recently, Dr. Wu joins the editorial board of Hospital Practices and Research since April 19, 2017. Further, on March 5, 2018, Dr. Wu joins the editorial board of SAR Journal. Beginning November 16, 2012, Dr. Wu becomes a senior member of International Economics Development and Research Center (IEDRC) as well as a senior member of International Association of Computer Science and Information Technology. Later, Dr. Wu becomes a fellow member of IEDRC since March 8, 2013 as well as a fellow member of IACSIT since March 26, 2018. On April 29, 2014, Dr. Wu was invited to be a keynote speaker of a three-joint international conference held in Taichung, Taiwan. In addition, Dr. Wu has become a distinguished professor of National Changhua University of Education notified on September 11, 2014. Further, Dr. Wu has received a lifetime exemption evaluation of National Changhua University of Education beginning from August 2015. On June 23, 2016, Dr. Wu was invited again to be a keynote speaker at 2016 3rd International Conference on Innovations in Business and Management (ICIBM 2016) and was an invited speaker in 45th Anniversary Activity of National Changhua University of Education in Academic Lecture on October 19, 2016. Moreover, on April 21, 2017, Dr. Wu was invited as a plenary speaker at 2nd International Multidisciplinary Research Conference (IMRC 2017). Dr. Wu also acted as a conference chair and keynote speaker at 2017 International Conference on E-Business and Internet (ICEBI 2017) on May 26, 2017. On October 26, 2018, Dr. Wu was invited to be a keynote speaker at 2018 International Conference on Education and Technology (ICET4). Dr. Wu acts as a lead guest editor of INQUIRY (indexed in Social Science Citation Index) in 2016-2017. Finally, Dr. Wu received his Distinguished Professorship (second term) from National Changhua University of Education since August 2017. For more information, please click here.
